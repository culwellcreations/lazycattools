How to Use the Random Team Generator

Enter your list of names — one per line or comma-separated — choose how many teams you want, and hit Generate Teams. The tool splits your group randomly in seconds. Use Reshuffle to try a different arrangement, or Share Link to send the results to your group.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work with uneven numbers?

Yes. If your group doesn't divide evenly, the extra people are distributed one at a time across teams. You'll never get an empty team.

How many teams can I make?

You can split into 2–10 teams using the number selector. You'll need at least as many names as teams.

Can I upload a list instead of typing?

Yes — use the Upload .txt or .csv button to load a file. Each name should be on its own line, or you can use a comma-separated format.

What are random team names?

Toggle Random team names to give each team a generated name (like "Team Falcon") instead of Team 1, Team 2, etc. — useful for games, trivia nights, or any time you want a bit more personality.

Is this tool free?

Completely free, forever. No account, no sign-up, no limits.

Who uses a random team generator?

Teachers splitting students into groups, coaches assigning players to sides, office managers running team-building activities, trivia hosts dividing tables, and anyone running a group game who wants a fair, unbiased split.
