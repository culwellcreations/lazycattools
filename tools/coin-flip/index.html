How to Use the Online Coin Flip

Hit Flip Coin and get heads or tails instantly. The tool tracks your running total of flips, heads, and tails so you can see how the results stack up over time. Hit Reset Stats to start a fresh session.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the coin flip truly random?

Yes. Each flip uses your browser's built-in random number generator — a 50/50 chance every time, with no memory of previous flips.

Why does it track heads and tails over multiple flips?

The stats bar is there if you want to flip multiple times and keep score — useful for games, settling a series of decisions, or just satisfying curiosity about how random the results really are.

Why use an online coin flip instead of a real coin?

No coin in your pocket, need a quick decision on your phone, or you're in a virtual meeting and need everyone to see the result — all good reasons to flip online. It's also handy when you want to flip multiple times and track the score automatically.

What can I use a coin flip for?

Deciding who goes first in a game, splitting a tie, choosing between two options, picking who pays for lunch, settling a friendly dispute — any situation where you need a fair, unbiased 50/50 decision.

Is this tool free?

Completely free — no sign-up, no account, no limits.
